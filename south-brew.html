Our Story

Started with one drum roaster and a folding table.

South Brew began in a garage with a 1kg sample roaster and a promise: never sell a bag of beans we wouldn't drink ourselves. Six years later, the roaster's bigger, the tables are wooden, but the promise hasn't moved.

We source direct from three smallholder co-ops, roast twice a week in-house, and pull every espresso shot fresh — nothing sits under a heat lamp here.
